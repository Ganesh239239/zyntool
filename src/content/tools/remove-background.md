## Make Backgrounds Transparent Instantly
Do you have a logo with an annoying white background? ZynTool's background remover uses **Color-Key Extraction** to isolate your subject and create a clean, transparent PNG.

### How to get the best results?
For the best results, use images with a solid white or single-color background. Adjust the **Sensitivity Slider** if the tool removes too much or too little of the background.
